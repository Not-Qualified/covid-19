List of Govt. Approved Labs For COVID-19 Testing

The central government expanded the network of government laboratories designated to test samples of suspected coronavirus disease (Covid-19) cases to 1132 laboratories. Among 1132 government laboratories, 502 are approved to test for Real-Time PCR (RT PCR) tests, 595 for TrueNat screening tests, and 35 for CBNAAT testing. Also, additional 905 private laboratories were approved to conduct COVID-19 tests. Among 905 private laboratories, 577 are approved to test RT PCR tests, 233 to test for TrueNat tests and 95 to test for CBNAAT testing.

Source: Government Testing Centers; Private Testing Centers; Government Labs Suitable For Testing
